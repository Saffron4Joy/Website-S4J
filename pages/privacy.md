## Introduction

At Saffron for Joy ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website saffronforjoy.com.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Contact Information:** When you use our contact form or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your name and email address.
- **Communications:** Any messages or content you send us through our contact form.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data:** We may collect information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited and time spent on pages.
- **Cookies:** We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Send you our newsletter (if you've subscribed)
- Respond to your inquiries
- Improve our website and content
- Analyze website usage to enhance user experience

## Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect information:
- **Formspree:** For contact form submissions
- **Google Analytics:** For website analytics (if enabled)
- **Newsletter Provider:** For managing email subscriptions

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
- Request access to your personal data
- Request deletion of your personal data
- Opt-out of analytics tracking

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@saffronforjoy.com.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
