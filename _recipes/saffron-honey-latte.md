servings: 2
difficulty: Easy

ingredients:
  - 1/8 teaspoon saffron threads (about 8-10 threads)
  - 2 tablespoons hot water
  - 2 cups milk (dairy or oat milk works beautifully)
  - 2 tablespoons honey (or maple syrup)
  - 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  - 1/8 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - Pinch of cinnamon for garnish

instructions:
  - "Bloom the saffron by adding threads to 2 tablespoons hot water. Let steep for 10 minutes while you prepare the other ingredients."
  - "In a small saucepan, gently warm the milk over medium-low heat. Do not let it boil."
  - "Add the honey and cardamom to the warm milk, stirring until honey dissolves."
  - "Pour in the bloomed saffron along with its soaking liquid. Add vanilla extract."
  - "Continue to warm for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally. The milk will turn a beautiful golden color."
  - "Optional: Use a milk frother to create a creamy foam."
  - "Pour into two mugs and dust with a pinch of cinnamon. Enjoy warm."

## A Golden Cup of Comfort

This saffron honey latte has become a beloved ritual in my home. There's something deeply comforting about wrapping your hands around a warm mug of this golden drink, inhaling its subtle honey-floral aroma.

## The Wellness Benefits

Beyond its delicious taste, this latte combines ingredients with genuine wellness benefits:

- **Saffron** - May help improve mood and promote relaxation
- **Honey** - Natural sweetener with antimicrobial properties
- **Cardamom** - Aids digestion and adds a wonderful aromatic quality

## When to Enjoy

This latte is perfect for:
- Quiet morning moments before the day begins
- An afternoon pick-me-up (it's naturally caffeine-free!)
- Evening relaxation before bed
- Whenever you need a moment of calm and comfort

The beautiful golden color comes entirely from the saffron - no artificial colors needed. It's like drinking liquid sunshine.
